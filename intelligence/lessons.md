# Learned Lessons

## Sports Markets
- In-play sports markets move violently — avoid tokens below 15¢
- Never buy both YES and NO on the same market across cycles
- Sports require edge > 0.15, not the standard 0.07

## General
- Consensus beats single opinion — require 2/3 swarm agreement before trading
- Unrealized losses count toward drawdown — mark-to-market matters
- Stop losses should fire once, not repeatedly — check for open sell orders first
