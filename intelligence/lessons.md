# Learned Lessons

## Sports Markets — DO NOT TRADE
- Sports markets are a consistent loss center: -$800+ across NBA, NHL, soccer, college sports
- The bot has NO edge in sports: LLM "high confidence" on favorites just agrees with market pricing
- In-play markets move on real-time scores — 15-min cycle arrives after the move
- Lottery ticket sports picks (3.5¢, 8¢) are pure capital destruction — base rates already priced
- **Rule: PASS on ALL sports markets, regardless of apparent edge or news signal**

## Macro/Geopolitical Markets — STRONG EDGE
- Iran conflict markets: consistent winners — news cycle has genuine 1-3 hour lag vs market
- Commodity NO markets (crude oil, gold): thesis-driven, weeks of runway, strong edge
- Fed/political resolution markets: moderate edge when catalyst is confirmed

## Sizing & Re-entry
- Never re-enter the same market within 24 hours — fragments position, wastes spread (Fed market lesson)
- Buying opposite side of existing position destroys capital (side-conflict bug)
- Stop losses should fire once — check for open sell orders before placing new ones

## Edge Quality
- "I agree with the market" is NOT edge — market already priced the consensus view
- "I can imagine this happening" is NOT edge — base rate already reflected in price
- Real edge = "I have a specific catalyst the market hasn't repriced yet"
- Consensus beats single opinion — require 2/3 source agreement before trading

## Price Level Guards
- NO price > 0.88: skip — collecting 12¢ premium on tail risk not worth blow-up
- YES price < 0.08: skip unless confirmed breaking catalyst directly enabling outcome
- In-play sports tokens below 15¢: never touch

## General
- Unrealized losses count toward drawdown — mark-to-market matters
- Geopolitical and commodity markets are the profit engine — allocate there
