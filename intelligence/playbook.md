# Trading Playbook

## Market Selection
- Prioritize: macro/political markets (Iran, crude oil, elections)
- Secondary: crypto price markets
- Caution: sports markets (cap at $30, require 15% edge)
- Avoid: in-play markets with token price < 15¢

## Position Sizing
- NORMAL mode: $40-150 per trade, Kelly-scaled
- RECOVERY mode (DD>10%): $25-75 per trade
- EXPANSION mode (new high): $75-200 per trade
- Sports cap: $30 regardless of mode
